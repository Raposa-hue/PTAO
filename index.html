About

Rooted in dance. Shaped by the moment.

Paulo Travassos and Azak Okena are a live electronic duo from Portugal, now based in Besançon, France. Built around guitar, drums and loopstation, their music is rooted in dance — hypnotic, techno-inspired and shaped entirely by the moment. No setlists. No rules. Just the floor.

The Duo

Meet P.T.A.O.

Guitar / Loopstation

Paulo Travassos

The sonic architect. Paulo's guitar and loopstation build layered, ambient textures in real time — weaving hypnotic loops into an ever-evolving dancefloor-ready groove.

Drums

Azak Okena

The rhythmic engine. Azak's live drumming drives the groove with raw physical energy — relentless, human and locked into a hypnotic techno-inspired pulse.
